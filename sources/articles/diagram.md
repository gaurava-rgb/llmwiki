## **2 - The list of the best prompts to create infographics with AI.**

I will go through 9 use cases of Nano-Banana-Pro for infographics, with prompts.

#### **1 - Create an infographic from [source].**

It’s the classic.

Prompt: **“Create an infographic on [topic], in a minimalistic (black and white) but ultra-detailed style.”**

For example, **“Create an infographic on the academic paper, in a minimalistic (black and white) but ultra-detailed style: UNIVERSE OF THOUGHTS: ENABLING CREATIVE”**

Image: <https://ibb.co/tpfv0w41>.

#### **2 - From YouTube to Infographic.**

First, go to YouTube and copy the link of a video.

Then copy and paste this:

Prompt: **“Analyze this video and its learnings: [youtube link].**

**We will then use your analysis to create an infographic out of it.”**

Once done, do this.

Prompt: **“Now create an infographic on this YouTube video. Be as detailed as possible, with a sketch style. Avoid futuristic style.”**

Image: <https://ibb.co/F4w5wB8s>.

#### **3 - Complex infographics.**

Prompt: **“Make an infographic that explains how the transformer LLM works.”**

Image: <https://ibb.co/8GH907t>.

Prompt: **“Make an infographic that explains how the quantum effects work in nature.”**

Image: <https://ibb.co/V0Zntzyp>.

Prompt: **“Make an infographic that explains the functioning of the heart.”**

Image: <https://ibb.co/fzP0PbXS>.

#### **4 - Complex prompting works too.**

Prompt: **“A step-by-step tutorial graphic titled ‘Secret Hacks for Confident Speaking”. Create a clean, flat-design infographic with four steps, each inside rounded boxes: (1) Embrace the silence before starting, (2) Reframe anxiety as excitement, (3) Lower vocal pitch at sentence ends, (4) Lock eyes with one person at a time. Use minimal vector-style illustrations, deep navy and energetic coral color palette, clear sans-serif English text, simple icons, and a modern layout. Include a large header banner at the top and numbered steps below. White background, high contrast, 16:9 aspect ratio, high-resolution 4K. Be as detailed as possible.”**

#### **5 - Your Linkedin profile, as the Lord of the Rings.**

Kudos to [Nate](<https://open.substack.com/users/119476445-nate>) for this [prompt](<https://substack.com/@natesnewsletter/note/c-180184887>).

> I made this Lord of the Rings themed map of Andrej Karpathy’s career path in about 2 minutes in Nano Banana Pro.
>
> Here’s how:
>
>   1. Grab a PDF of your LinkedIn profile.
>
>   2. Hit Nano Banana Pro with the PDF (I recommend AI Studio for 4k resolution).
>
>   3. Ask it to please create a fun career map of your resume using X theme, and specifically ask it to include relevant allusions or metaphors that connect your resume to your chosen theme, so the career map feels coherent. Remind Nano Banana Pro to write all text legibly and clearly.
>
>

>
> And that’s it!

#### **9 - The mega prompt.**

This is the most complete prompt I could make.

I am making a video using it down below, while writing a Linkedin post alongside it. FYI, I reach over a million impressions per day on Linkedin.

> Design a [ASPECT RATIO] infographic.
>
> ## SUBJECT MATTER
>
> Topic: [TOPIC]
>
> Central Focus: [MAIN IDEA]
>
> ## VISUAL STYLE
>
> Art Style: [SPECIFIC STYLE NAME]
>
> Color Palette: [PRIMARY COLORS] + [ACCENT COLOR]
>
> Background: [BACKGROUND TYPE]
>
> ## COMPOSITION & LAYOUT
>
> Structure: [LAYOUT TYPE]
>
> Elements: [SECONDARY ELEMENTS/ICONS]
