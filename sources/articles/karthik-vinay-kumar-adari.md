Most job seekers make one mistake:

They only apply on company websites and job portals.

But some of the best opportunities never reach that stage.

A lot of roles get filled through 𝘀𝘁𝗮𝗳𝗳𝗶𝗻𝗴 𝗳𝗶𝗿𝗺𝘀 before most people even know they exist.

I’ve seen so many candidates spend weeks applying online, refreshing LinkedIn, and wondering why nothing is moving.

Then one conversation with the right recruiter changes everything.

A good staffing firm can do more than just "share openings."

They can help you find roles that match your profile, push your resume faster, explain what the hiring team is actually looking for, and sometimes even get you into opportunities that were never publicly advertised.

That’s why I always say this:

If you’re serious about your job search, don’t ignore staffing firms.

Build real relationships with recruiters.  
Keep your resume ready.  
Reply on time.  
Be clear about the roles you want.

Sometimes it’s not about applying to 200 jobs.

Sometimes it’s about being visible to the right person at the right time.

And staffing firms can help make that happen.
