10 years ago this kid casually sent me his resume via Craigslist.

He'd just finished school, with no experience or real estate knowledge.

I needed someone to cut checks and show space, so I called him in for an interview.

This is James Martin, and here's what happened next:

![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/Gt996XPWAAE7p6R.jpg)![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/Gt9-EYtXsAAceJk.png)

* * *

He arrived at our office early, and for some reason brought a novel to read while he waited in the lobby.

I thought it was a tad out of the ordinary, but the interview was good enough that I gave him his first test:

Join me for a full day property tour.

He took me up on it.

* * *

I spent the day showing him some of our properties -- and he was mostly quiet.

You usually get a "thank you, I would love to discuss next steps" email as a follow up.

This was what he sent instead:

![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/Gt-B0kEXMAAEyaP.jpg)

* * *

He was hired.

When he started, I threw him right into the fire.

Although he had no construction experience, I told him he was also going to oversee two big remodels.

With zero hesitation, he started studying.

Here's the crew -- confused why this kid was in charge.

![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/Gt-EKAkXcAAPaea.jpg)![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/Gt-EMR9WMAAZveb.jpg)![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/Gt-E40uW4AEm3dj.jpg)

* * *

He quickly learned how to read plans, review budgets, work with city inspectors, understand the merits of change orders, and more importantly -- the art of bugging people enough so they show up and meet their deadlines.

He started gaining confidence.

![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/Gt-Fh52XgAA9sy_.jpg)![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/Gt-Fl5RWQAAbMLV.jpg)

* * *

In the meantime, we were becoming great friends.

We've traveled around the world, gotten to know each other's families -- and he was even there with a whole Zachary’s pizza (my wife's favorite) at the beach right after she said "YES"

![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/Gt-GJWLXMAATohI.jpg)![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/Gt-GL0aXQAAH4Is.jpg)![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/Gt-HLS-WYAAb7tv.jpg)![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/Gt-IG5pWUAAsPr-.jpg)

* * *

We've been through some incredible milestones together - including passing $100M in capital raised.

Here we are meeting with our lead securities lawyer from DLA Piper right when we were about the launch the current fund (2022).

![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/Gt-I96qW0AAB6_I.jpg)

* * *

It's been an incredible and wild ride my friend, and I cannot wait for the many great things ahead.

Happy 10 years -- I love you man!

![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/Gt-JPWXXsAATEac.jpg)![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/Gt-JYyJWUAAN53J.jpg)
