Your mind is a concept graph, it works with analogies (edges)

Conversations are tree walks (mostly linear, sometimes you recurse down paths and have to find your way back up)

Your (agents) knowledge base must be a concept graph.

You should interface it with stories on demand

This.

Think of it as communication.

Your mind is the full graph and you communicate in paths to reconstruct the graph in another person.

Humans take in linear stories not graphs.

Same for the vault.

The agent vault is the full graph.

You don't need to read it (although you can).

Let the agent tell you linear stories for you to learn.

These can be in context to what you need right now.

You still need the mind, you still need the graph. That's the substance on which we communicate.
