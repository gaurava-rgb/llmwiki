🅰️ ℹ️ Hiring or interviewing for AI PM roles right now. This Rubric is a cheat sheet to hire the right person and get hired in the AI PM role

1️⃣ Ecosystem sense 🌍

🔷 Can they map model, infra, and tooling players—and explain implications for a roadmap and build/buy choices.
🔷 Do they know what would make development 10x faster: evaluation tooling, feedback loops, and dev‑infra collaboration.
🔷 Are they tracking market shifts and constraints (open vs proprietary, chips, cost‑to‑serve).

2️⃣ Concepts that matter 🧠

🔷 Explain LLMs in plain language: tokens, embeddings, probabilistic outputs.
🔷 Choose RAG vs fine‑tuning intentionally and improve post‑launch with data, prompts, and iteration.
🔷 Reason about latency, context windows, and hallucinations as product tradeoffs.

3️⃣ Product, trust, and scale 🚀

🔷 Human‑in‑the‑loop by design with examples where user input shaped model behavior.
🔷 Trust‑building UX patterns: citations, transparency, staged interactions.
🔷Evidence of shipped impact plus pricing/GTM clarity and scalability thinking.

4️⃣ Collaboration and restraint 🤝

🔷 Shared vocabulary with ML/data teams; balances product goals with model constraints.
🔷 Knows when AI is not the answer and scopes lean, low‑risk MVPs
