Got 2nd place in the [@GoogleDeepMind](<https://twitter.com/GoogleDeepMind>) SG hackathon!

I built a webapp that converts 2d topo maps into 3d colourful renders + lets you fly through and ask gemini live api what you're looking at.

Your browser does not support the video tag.

* * *

1\. download any [@USGS](<https://twitter.com/USGS>) topo map (.tif or .jpg)
2\. pull elevation from [@awscloud](<https://twitter.com/awscloud>) terrain tiles
3\. [@NanoBanana](<https://twitter.com/NanoBanana>) pro (via [@fal](<https://twitter.com/fal>)) to colour the maps
4\. hop on the plane with [@GeminiApp](<https://twitter.com/GeminiApp>) live api as your tour guide
