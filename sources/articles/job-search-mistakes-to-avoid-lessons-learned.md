What I’d do differently if I had to job search from scratch today.

A few I’d fix immediately:

• I relied too heavily on job boards
I’d still apply, but I’d spend equal time on referrals and direct outreach.

• I waited too long to talk to people
I’d start conversations early, before I “felt ready.”

• I treated networking like a favor
I’d approach it as curiosity, not asking for help.

• I prepared answers, not stories
I’d build 3–4 strong examples I could reuse across interviews.

• I optimized my resume, not my positioning
I’d focus more on showing decisions and outcomes.

None of this is complicated.
It’s just rarely explained clearly.
