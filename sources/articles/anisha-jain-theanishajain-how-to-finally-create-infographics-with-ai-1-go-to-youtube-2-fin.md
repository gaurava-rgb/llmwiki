**How to (finally) create infographics with AI:**

1\. Go to YouTube

2\. Find a long lecture. Copy the URL.

3\. Paste it in Gemini with this prompt:

"_Analyse deeply the content of this video [YT URL]_ "

Let Gemini process the video.

5\. Prompt Gemini again:

"_Generate an infographic. Turn this transcript into a cheatsheet with key takeaways and give final output as 9:16 image_ "

This is how you turn any video in a visual summary.
