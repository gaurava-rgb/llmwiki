lifehack nobody talks about:

Obsidian + Claude Code = your second brain becomes smarter than you

[@internetvin](<https://twitter.com/internetvin>) made 12 custom clash commands (in [@gregisenberg](<https://twitter.com/gregisenberg>)'s podcast)

some examples:

/trace → track how an idea evolved over months

/ghost → answer questions in YOUR voice

/emerge → surface patterns you're circling but haven't named

the big takeaway here is that "the quality of context you feed the agent determines what it can do for you"
