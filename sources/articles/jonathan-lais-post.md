come work with me at [Andreessen Horowitz](<https://www.linkedin.com/company/a16z?trk=public_post-text>)! we're hiring a partner on our Speedrun investment team - this is a small, fast-growing team inside a16z focused on first-money-in investing in brand new AI start-ups building at the frontier

if you’re technical and love working hands-on with founders at the 0-1 stage, come talk to us or refer a friend we should meet!

since launch, [a16z speedrun](<https://www.linkedin.com/school/a16zspeedrun/?trk=public_post-text>) has invested in over 200 startups with $300M+ in capital deployed. alumni include [FLORA](<https://www.linkedin.com/company/floraai?trk=public_post-text>), [Runware](<https://www.linkedin.com/company/runware?trk=public_post-text>), [Fundamental Research Labs](<https://www.linkedin.com/company/shortcutai?trk=public_post-text>), [Hedra](<https://www.linkedin.com/company/hedra-labs?trk=public_post-text>), [k-ID](<https://sg.linkedin.com/company/k-id?trk=public_post-text>), [Rork](<https://www.linkedin.com/company/rork-app?trk=public_post-text>), [Nexxa.ai](<https://www.linkedin.com/company/nexxa-ai?trk=public_post-text>) and many more. we recently raised a fresh new fund to back the next generation of founders

our investment partners help identify and select the companies we invest in - thinking critically about new technologies and market trends. they also serve as mentors for our founders, helping them make progress across product, technology, GTM, fundraising, etc

a few things we look for:
\- experience as a builder (engineering, product etc) or an investor
\- technical / passion for AI - you've embraced AI in your workflows and are passionate about optimizing your coding agent’s performance, with a side project (or two) in flight
\- interest in start-ups - founder empathy is a key a16z cultural value. you've either founded a start-up, worked at one, or are contributing on the side
\- social network - you're at home conversing with other builders, and might be active on X, LinkedIn, or Substack

please send a note to [speedruninvesting@a16z.com](<mailto:speedruninvesting@a16z.com?trk=public_post-text>) with your Linkedin and a link to a product or terminal session you’re proud of creating. we can't wait to meet you!
