## **STEP 2: THE LEAD MAGNET POST STRUCTURE**


now here's where strategy meets execution.

we're not just making a regular post. **we're creating a lead magnet post** that forces people to raise their hand and identify themselves as high-intent prospects.

**THE ANATOMY OF A 2,000+ COMMENT POST**

every viral trendjacking post we create follows this exact formula:

**COMPONENT #1: THE NEWSFLASH HOOK**

your first line needs to accomplish two things:

1\. **contextualize what just happened** (the launch/announcement)

1\. **make a bold claim** about what it means

examples from our actual posts that generated 800-2,000 comments each:

**"google just released gemini 3. LLMs have officially entered the race to run your entire business."**

**"gemini 3 just launched and is destroying chatgpt on every benchmark. 99% of marketers are still overpaying for worse results."**

**"BREAKING: gemini 3 just got released and it can now do the work of a $200k/year GTM consultant."**

notice the pattern:

\- **[con**text] </stron\</strong>g> \+ [bold claim]****

\- **[what happ**ened]** \+ [why it mat**ters to your ICP]

the hook needs to stop the scroll. it needs to make people think "wait, what is this and why do i need to know about it?"

**COMPONENT #2: THE VALUE OVERVIEW**

now you need to tell them **what you're giving away.**

and this is where most people fumble: they don't make it valuable enough or easy enough.

here's the framework:

**"i just spent [X hours] building [specific resource] that lets you [specific outcome] in [short timeframe]"**

real examples:

"i just spent 6 hours building 15 copy-paste prompts that let you generate qualified B2B leads using gemini 3 in under 10 minutes"

"i created a complete prompt library that does the work of a $200k/year GTM consultant - content strategy, outreach sequences, positioning frameworks - all automated"

"i built a notion doc with 20 gemini 3 prompts that write cold emails that book meetings at a 40%+ response rate"

**what makes this work:**

1\. **time investment anchor** \- "spent 6 hours building this" = high perceived value

1\. **specificity** \- not "some prompts," but "15 copy-paste prompts"

1\. **clear outcome** \- "generate qualified B2B leads"

1\. **speed benefit** \- "in under 10 minutes"

1\. **comparison to expensive alternative** \- "does the work of a $200k consultant"

you're basically saying: _"i already did the hard work. you just need to copy-paste and get results immediately."_

**that's irresistible.**

**COMPONENT #3: THE FRICTIONLESS CTA**

this is where you capture the lead.

**keep it brain-dead simple:**

"comment GEMINI below and i'll DM you the link"

"comment PROMPTS and i'll send it over"

"drop a SEND below and make sure we're connected so i can message you"

**why this works:**

\- **low friction** \- commenting is easier than filling out a form

\- **instant gratification** \- they get it via DM within minutes

\- **network effect** \- every comment pushes the post to that person's entire network

when someone comments on your post, linkedin distributes **your post + their comment** to all of their connections.

**each commenter becomes a distribution channel.**

if 1,000 people comment, your post gets syndicated across 1,000 different networks.

**that's how you hit 500k+ impressions in 24 hours.**

**COMPONENT #4: THE VISUAL ASSET**

here's the easiest part: **steal the official graphic from the company's announcement.**

when google launches gemini 3, they release branded graphics. when openai launches gpt-5, they have official visuals.

**just use those.**

why this works:

1\. **brand recognition** \- people instantly recognize the google/openai/anthropic branding

1\. **credibility transfer** \- associating your post with a major brand = instant authority

1\. **visual contrast** \- minimalist corporate branding stands out in a feed full of text posts

1\. **zero design work** \- you literally just screenshot and upload

go to twitter, find the announcement tweet, right-click the image, save it, upload to linkedin.

**done.**
